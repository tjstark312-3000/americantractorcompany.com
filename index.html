The Problem

American farming is running on empty.

"Finding reliable help for planting and harvest is the biggest thing keeping me up at night. Not yields, not prices — people."

— Iowa corn farmer, 3,200 acres · ATC field research, 2026

The American farmer feeds the world. But the industry is in a slow-motion crisis that Silicon Valley keeps ignoring. Labor is drying up. Input costs are through the roof. Equipment from the legacy OEMs costs a quarter-million dollars and still doesn't think for itself.

The technology to fix this exists. It just hasn't been pointed at the right problem yet — until now.

  • 01
    The help isn't coming

    Farm labor availability dropped 30% since 2015. H-2A visa delays leave crops in the field. Farmers can't build a business around a workforce that might not show up.

  • 02
    Precision tools that don't connect

    GPS guidance, yield monitors, soil sensors — all separate apps talking to no one. None of it makes decisions. The farmer still has to do that alone at midnight.

  • 03
    Legacy OEMs built for yesterday

    John Deere, CNH, AGCO — great iron, but their autonomy is bolt-on GPS auto-steer with a marketing budget. No real reasoning. No adaptation. No intelligence.

  • 04
    Time is the most valuable crop

    Planting windows are 10 days. Harvest is unforgiving. Every missed pass, every breakdown, every late start costs real money. Farmers don't need smarter dashboards — they need the work done.

Our Solution

We built the intelligence first. Then the tractor.

Most autonomy startups start with the machine and bolt on AI. We did it the other way. AgFM-1 is a foundation model trained exclusively on agricultural data — soil chemistry, weather, crop biology, machine telemetry, satellite imagery. The tractor is purpose-built to express it.

01AgFM-1 Foundation ModelCore IP+
The world's first multimodal AI foundation model trained exclusively on agricultural data. Soil composition, crop phenology, weather systems, equipment telemetry, satellite and hyperspectral imagery — unified into one model that understands your farm and acts on it faster than any operator can.
What this means for you

Your tractor reads the field like your most experienced hand — adjusting depth, speed, and spacing in real time based on what it actually senses underfoot, not what the map says.

02Full Sensor Suite & PerceptionPrototyping+
Custom sensor fusion: LiDAR, multispectral cameras, RTK GNSS/IMU, ground-penetrating soil probes, and obstacle detection — all feeding AgFM-1 in real time so the machine understands what's happening above and below the soil simultaneously.
What this means for you

It sees the waterway before you hit it. It knows a rock from a clod. It won't run into a deer at 2am — and it won't stop for shadows either.

03Autonomous Tractor AgentIn Design+
Mission planning, adaptive path optimization, implement control, and in-field exception handling — all orchestrated by the agent layer on top of AgFM-1. The tractor reasons about its task end to end, from loading the planter to shutting down at the fence line.
What this means for you

Set the field, choose the job, go to bed. Wake up to it done. No babysitting. No radio checks. No fatigue errors at hour 16.

04ATC Tractor PlatformDesign Phase+
Purpose-built chassis, drivetrain, and implement interface designed from the first principle that no human will ever sit in this seat. Optimized for sensor placement, compute power delivery, and serviceability in your shop with standard tools — no internet required.
What this means for you

Built tough, built to maintain. Compatible with standard 3-point implements and PTO. Your existing planter, disc, and sprayer hook right up.

05Fleet Intelligence & Farm DashboardRoadmap+
Remote oversight, fleet management, agronomic analytics, and continuous model updates delivered over-the-air. Every acre worked makes the model smarter — for you and every farmer on the network. A data flywheel no competitor can buy their way into.
What this means for you

One simple app. Know where every machine is, what it's done, what it flagged. From your kitchen table or the co-op parking lot.
